Organic Veggie Rice

Description

Organic jasmine rice with a tangy-sweet blend of healthy organic veggie flavors and a hint of umami that is irresistible down to the last bite. high in protein and vitamins from egg, mushroom, peppers and onion, and healthy cooking oils, this meal will satisfy your calorie needs in a healthy and balanced way.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Lundberg Family Farms organic white jasmine rice
  • 13⁄4 cups filtered water
  • 2 organic red bell peppers
  • 1 organic orange bell pepper
  • 1⁄4 organic red onion
  • 6 organic baby bella mushrooms
  • 1 bunch cilantro
  • 2 organic or pasture raised extra-large brown eggs
  • 2 tsp sememe oil (toasted or untoasted)
  • 1 tsp chili flakes or chili oil
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 1⁄2 tsp sea salt
  • powdered white pepper

Steps

  1. Wash measured rice under running water with a thin strainer for 1 minute and drain water
  2. Add measured seseme oil, chili flakes or chili oil, and olive oil to a pot or rice cooker
  3. Add rice
  4. Add measured sea salt
  5. Add measured water
  6. Start rice cooker or cover pot and bring to a light simmer
  7. Set timer to 15 minutes after which shut off heat on rice and let sit, covered for 10 minutes
  8. While rice is cooking, wash and dice bell peppers small
  9. Add olive oil and diced peppers to a medium sized cast iron or stainless steel skillet or wok. add heat
  10. While stirring the sauté as needed, wash soil off organic mushrooms, cut off the tip of each stem, dice them and add them to sauté
  11. Dice 1⁄4 organic red onion and add to sauté
  12. Once sauté is to perfection, transfer into pot with finished rice and sprinkle some powdered white pepper on top
  13. Scramble 2 eggs in the same skillet or wok to incorporate the flavors of the sauté, sprinkle with ground white pepper, and add to rice pot
  14. wash and chop cilantro and add to rice, stir it all together
  15. serve into dishes and enjoy!
