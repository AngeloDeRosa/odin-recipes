Veggie-rice

Description

Organic jasmine rice with a tangy-sweet blend of healthy organic veggie flavors and a hint of umami that is irresistible down to the last bite. high in protein and vitamins from egg, mushroom, peppers and onion, and healthy cooking oils, this meal will satisfy your calorie needs in a healthy and balanced way.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Lundberg Family Farms organic white jasmine rice
  • 13⁄4 cups filtered water
  • 2 organic red bell peppers
  • 1 organic orange bell pepper
  • 1⁄4 organic red onion
  • 6 organic baby bella mushrooms
  • 1 bunch cilantro
  • 2 organic or pasture raised extra-large brown eggs
  • 2 tsp sememe oil (toasted or untoasted)
  • 1 tsp chili flakes or chili oil
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 1⁄2 tsp sea salt
  • powdered white pepper
